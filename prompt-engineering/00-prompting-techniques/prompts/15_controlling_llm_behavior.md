# 15 — Controlling LLM Behavior

## What it means

Controlling LLM behavior means shaping how the model responds.

## Main idea

The same information can be expressed in very different ways.

Behavior control helps define:
- tone
- style
- level of detail
- neutrality
- professionalism
- response boundaries

## Why this matters

Without behavior control, the model may:
- sound too casual
- sound too technical
- include unnecessary detail
- drift away from the communication goal

With behavior control, the output becomes more suitable for the real use case.

## Example use case

An executive briefing assistant should:
- sound professional
- stay concise
- focus on progress, business impact, and risks
- avoid unnecessary jargon

## Key idea

Controlling LLM behavior helps shape the style and focus of the answer, not just the content.
